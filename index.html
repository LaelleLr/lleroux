The sunset mindset

      

I have always been more of a night owl than an early bird. This used to be something I wanted to change about myself but I as grow old I learn to embrace it. I chose to see the advantages and the beauty of the "evening energy". I like to call it the sunset mindset.

      

The idea is to see the evening as a window between the day and the night. A blissful moment to reflect on the day and see all that the night has to offer. Just like the sunset is a beautiful transition between the day and the night, the sunset mindset is the ability to embrace what is gone and what is to come with serenity and to create energy from it.
